About CGN Traffic Center

CGN Traffic Center is a reader-service page for Illinois drivers, Chicago commuters, families, event travelers and newsroom readers who need a fast place to check road conditions and official source links.

The Waze map is provided by Waze. Official Illinois road condition, construction and winter travel information should be checked through IDOT, Getting Around Illinois and related public transportation sources.

Important Safety Note

Do not operate this page, Waze or any traffic map while driving. Pull over safely, ask a passenger to check conditions, or review travel conditions before departure.

Traffic feeds, maps, cameras and crowdsourced reports may be delayed, incomplete, unavailable or affected by maintenance. During emergencies, follow law enforcement, emergency management, state transportation agencies and public-safety instructions.
